## Introduction

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed.

## Body Text

Good document typography serves a functional purpose first and foremost. Readers should be able to navigate a document effortlessly, following the hierarchy of headings without conscious effort, and reading body text for extended periods without fatigue.
